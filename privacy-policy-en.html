Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Bunker Mesh was built with a total focus on privacy and data sovereignty. This policy explains how we handle information.

1. Data Collection

We collect nothing. Bunker Mesh has no central servers. We do not ask for email, phone numbers, or any personal data. Your identity is generated locally via encryption.

2. Permissions

  • Bluetooth: To discover nearby devices (Mesh Network).
  • Location: Required by the system for Bluetooth to function. We do not track your GPS location.
  • Camera: Used exclusively to scan contact QR Codes.
  • Biometrics: Only for local app unlocking.

3. Encryption

All messages are end-to-end encrypted (E2EE) and stored only on your device.
